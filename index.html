Key Features:

The Michigan Township's Association Website is a substantial project involving the migration of over 300 pages and the implementation of key features. Key Features:

  1. Homepage Design: As the featured news posts cycle through, the animation is eye-catching and gives the homepage a unique and modern feel.
  2. Impexium Integration: Utilizing MTA's current Membership platform, Impexium, the website needed to verify every membership on login. In addition, many of the pages were filtered based on their level of access given in Impexium.
  3. Answer Center: The Member Only answer center is the focal point of the website, so it was important to optimize search and maintainability. A custom Wordpress module was created for admin users to manage the Answer Center effectively.

The redevelopment of the Michigan Township's Association Website was a multifaceted initiative aimed at modernization and enhanced functionality. Our team strategically redesigned the homepage with animated news posts for an engaging visual experience. A highlight of the project is the seamless integration of the Impexium API, ensuring secure membership verification and personalized content access. The Member Only Answer Center, a central hub for exclusive resources, was optimized with a custom WordPress module, offering administrators efficient control and members easy access. This project redefines the association's digital presence, combining aesthetics with advanced features for an elevated user experience.
