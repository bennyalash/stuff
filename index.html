The Clare County Service Request Plugin is a user-friendly web application designed to empower citizens to report and address various service requests seamlessly. Developed for the Clare County Road Commission, this application enhances community engagement by providing a convenient and efficient platform for residents to communicate their concerns regarding infrastructure and public services

Key Features:

  1. Custom Form: Citizens can easily access the web application and fill out a custom form tailored for reporting service requests. The intuitive design ensures a straightforward and user-friendly experience.
  2. Service Request Selection: Users can choose from a predefined list of service requests, including common issues like potholes, street light outages, tree removal, and more. This ensures accurate categorization of reported problems.

Benefits:

  • Community Empowerment: Citizens can actively contribute to the improvement of their community by easily reporting issues.
  • Efficient Administration: Administrators can manage and prioritize service requests through a centralized and user-friendly interface.

The Clare County Service Request Plugin stands as a testament to the power of technology in fostering collaboration between citizens and local government, ultimately contributing to the enhancement of public services and community well-being.
