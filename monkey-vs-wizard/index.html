Setup

Deal every player 3 Cards.

Discard cards from the deck. These should be set aside and not looked at. See the chart below to know how many cards to discard.

Place the deck in the middle of the playing area. Flip up two cards on the table. Whenever one is taken, replace it.

Gameplay

Instead of taking turns, all players will do things at the same time.

Each round, take 1 card from your hand and put it face down on the table.

Once every player is ready, flip over all of the cards.

Resolve each card in ORDER, based on the ORDER on the card. Start with the lowest ORDER and move up.

When it your "Turn" based on the ORDER, draw one card, either face-up or from the deck.

When a card is resolved, do the action written on the card.

If you have a passive action, make sure you follow the instructions throughout the round.

After all of the cards have been resolved, whichever card has the highest POWER wins. The base POWER is the number on the top left of the card.

If there is a tie for POWER, whichever has a higher ORDER wins.

Note: If a card ends the round early and declares a winner, do not continue to do the actions, but each player should still draw cards.

Winning

When all the cards run out, count your Victory Points in your pile. Whoever has the most points wins!

If you would like to play multiple rounds in one game, you can play to 50 Points and track points after each round. First to 50 wins!
