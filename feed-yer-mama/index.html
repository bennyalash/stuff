Setup

Deal every player 3 Cards and give every player 1 Coin.

Set all player markers at the start of the Feed 'Yer Mama Point Tracking Card.

Set the Feed ‘Yer Mama Food Board to the Start Space.

Everyone Rolls the Dice, Highest roller goes first

Give out bonus resources to each player depending on turn order:

Turn Order

Bonus Resources

Player 1

None

Player 2

1 Card

Player 3

1 Coin

Player 4

1 Food

Player 5

1 Card and 1 Coin

Your Turn

These steps must be done in order, but each step is optional.

  • GAIN 2 COINS or DRAW 1 CARD or GAIN 1 FOOD
  • USE YOUR CARDS' ABILITIES
    In any order and each is optional.
  • PLAY CARD(S)
    Pay for them using the cost in the top corners of the card. The top left is the coin cost and the top right is the food cost.
  • FEED 'YER MAMA
    Pay the current amount that is on the next space of the Feed ‘Yer Mama Food Board. Then, move your player and the marker up one space on the Feed 'Yer Mama Point Tracking Card. You may only do this once per turn.

Winning

If you reach the winning Space on the point tracking card, you have Fully Fed 'Yer Mama and you win the game!

The Winning Space depends on the number of players playing.

Rules & Exceptions

If you ever get to gain Coins and there are no Coins left, steal Coins from other players.

If you ever get to gain Food and there are no Food left, steal Food from other players. See Pig for Exception

If you ever get to draw cards and there are none left, do nothing.

If the cards run out, reshuffle all discarded, destroyed, and killed cards.

Abilities in italics are not part of each turn’s action, but something that is always true of the card.

Card Clarifications

Dog: The Wolf can kill the Dog. Once the Dog is dead, your other animals can also be killed. You can kill your own pig even if you have a dog.

Goat: You decide which plant to destroy AFTER rolling the die.

Corn: You cannot copy another player's animal. Only copy the part of the ability that is not italicized

Fox: You cannot copy one of your own animals. Only copy the part of the ability that is not italicized, for example if you copy the Dog, ignore the italics about your other animals.

Pig: Gain 1 Food and place it on the Pig card. Food here cannot be spent. It also cannot be stolen from the raccoon or from other players when the food runs out*. You can kill your own pig even if you have a dog.
*It can be stolen if that is the only way a player can gain food, meaning no one else has food

Wolf: You decide which animal to destroy after rolling the die. When deciding if you can kill an animal, ignore the food cost and only compare the dice roll with the Coin cost.

Advanced Rules

These Rules are recommended only after playing multiple times.

  • Rule Change #1.
    At the start of the game, only deal 2 cards to each player. Also, place 3 cards face-up in the middle of the playing area. Players may draw cards from here, when they do, replace them.

  • Rule Change #2.
    At the start of each turn, instead of doing step 1, roll the dice and gain temporary buying power equal to your dice roll to immediately spend on the following resources:

    Cost

    Resources

    1

    1 Coin

    2

    1 Card

    3

    1 Food

    4

    2 Coins

    5

    2 Cards

    6

    2 Food

    You cannot buy from the same row twice.

  • Rule Change #3.

    If you ever roll a 1 for any reason, steal 1 coin.

    If you ever roll a 6 for any reason, give another player 1 of your coins.
