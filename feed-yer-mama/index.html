Feed 'Yer Mama

Welcome to the village, Farmer! Life here is simple: grow crops, raise animals, and most importantly—keep your very hungry Mama fed. But fair warning: Mama isn’t just hungry—she’s insatiable. And the more you feed her, the hungrier she gets.

Each farmer has their own bottomless-pit Mama to satisfy, and this isn’t about sharing. You’ll need to out-farm and out-feed your neighbors to keep your Mama happy. Serve her everything, but don’t get too comfy. Just when you think you’re ahead, she’ll ask, “Is that all you’ve got?”

Feed your Mama the most, the fastest, and you’ll claim victory. The rest? Let’s just say their Mamas won’t let them hear the end of it.

So grab your pitchfork and get ready to Feed ’Yer Mama!
