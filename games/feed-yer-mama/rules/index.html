Feed 'Yer Mama

Game Contents

55 Cards

1 Rules Card

1 Point Tracking Card ('Yer Points)

1 How Much Mama Eats Card

24 Plant Cards (3 of Each)

28 Animal Cards (3 of Each, except only 1 Horse)

25 Hamburger Pieces

50 Coin Pieces

Setup

Deal every player 5 Cards.

Set all player markers at the start of the board and set the How Much Mama Eats to the Start Space (Next Space is 1 Food).

Everyone Rolls the Dice, Highest roller goes first...

Give the first player 1 Coin, the second player 2 Coins, the third player 3 Coins, and so on...

Start the Game

The first player takes their first turn (See Section E: Gameplay)

Winning

If you reach the winning Space on the point tracking card, you have Fully Fed 'Yer Mama!

The Winning Space depends on the number of players playing.

# of Times to Feed Mama:

  • 2 Players - Feed 'Yer Mama 6 Times
  • 3 Players - Feed 'Yer Mama 5 Times
  • 4 Players - Feed 'Yer Mama 4 Times
  • 5 Players - Feed 'Yer Mama 3 Times

Gameplay

Your Turn

THESE STEPS MUST BE DONE IN ORDER, BUT EACH STEP IS OPTIONAL.

1. GAIN 2 COINS or 1 CARD or 1 FOOD

2. USE YOUR CARDS' ABILITIES: In any order and each is optional.

3. PLAY CARD(S): Pay for them using the cost in the top corners of the card. The top left is the coin cost and the top right is the food cost.

4. KILL 1 OF YOUR ANIMALS: Pay the Coin Cost in the top left corner of the Animal. Place it on the Bottom of the Deck.

5. FEED 'YER MAMA: Pay the current amount that is on the next space of the Feed ‘Yer Mama Tracking Card. Then, move your player and the marker up one space. You may do this multiple times, but each time you must pay the new cost.

If at any point you have reached the # of Times to Feed Mama, you Win Immediately!

Other Rules / Explanations

If you ever get to gain Coins(s) and there are no Coins left, steal Coin(s) from the player with the most Coins. If you have the most Coins, do nothing.

If you ever get to gain food and there is no Food left, steal Food from the player with the most Food. If you have the most Food, do nothing. *See Pig for Exception

If you ever get to draw cards and there are none left, do nothing.

Abilities in italics are not part of each turn’s action, but something that is always true of the card.

Card Clarifications

Dog: The Wolf can kill the Dog. Once the Dog is dead, your other animals can also be killed.

Goat: You decide which plant to destroy after rolling the die. Example: You roll a die and get a 5. You can now destroy any player's, including your own, Cabbage, Carrot, Corn, or Onion. If you do destroy a plant and only if you do, gain 1 Food.

Fox: You cannot copy one of your own animals. You must do exactly as the card you are copying says., for example: Copying the Pig - If you choose the second option of placing 1 Food on the Pig, you either can put the Food on a Pig that you have or on an opponent’s pig, but you cannot put Food on the Fox. Only copy the part of the ability that is not italicized, for example if you copy the Horse, ignore the italics about opponents’s dice rolls.

Pig: The pig is the only exception to rule B.2. In the specific case where there is not enough food in the bank after killing the Pig, you do not take additional food from another player, even if there isn't enough for you.

Horse: All rolls for every opponent are now 1 less. This includes the Goat, Wolf, and Cow.

Wolf: You decide which animal to destroy after rolling the die. Example: You roll a die and get a 6. You can now destroy any player's animal, including your own, Cat, Chicken, Raccoon, Dog, Goat, Fox, or Pig (Assuming there is not a Horse in play). If you do destroy an animal and only if you do, gain 1 Food.

Cow: If you kill the Cow, by paying 7 Coins, immediately Feed ‘Yer Mama. Move your piece up one step and also move the How Much Mama Eats counter up one step.
