Feed 'Yer Mama

Game Contents

55 Cards

1 Rules Card

1 Point Tracking Card ('Yer Points)

1 How Much Mama Eats Card

24 Plant Cards (3 of Each)

28 Animal Cards (3 of Each, except only 1 Horse)

25 Hamburger Pieces

50 Coin Pieces

Setup

Deal Cards

Deal every player 5 Cards.

Set the Tracking Cards

Set all player markers at the start of the board and set the How Much Mama Eats to the Start Space (Next Space is 1 Food).

Starting Resources

Everyone Rolls the Dice, Highest roller goes first...

Give the first player 1 Coin, the second player 2 Coins, the third player 3 Coins, and so on...

Start the Game

The first player takes their first turn (See Section E: Gameplay)

Winning

If you reach the winning Space on the point tracking card, you have Fully Fed 'Yer Mama!

The Winning Space depends on the number of players playing.

# of Times to Feed Mama:

  • 2 Players - Feed 'Yer Mama 6 Times
  • 3 Players - Feed 'Yer Mama 5 Times
  • 4 Players - Feed 'Yer Mama 4 Times
  • 5 Players - Feed 'Yer Mama 3 Times

Gameplay

Your Turn

THESE STEPS MUST BE DONE IN ORDER, BUT EACH STEP IS OPTIONAL.

FARM

GAIN 2 COINS or 1 CARD or 1 FOOD

USE YOUR CARDS' ABILITIES

PLAY CARD(S)

KILL 1 OF YOUR ANIMALS

FEED 'YER MAMA

If at any point you have reached the # of Times to Feed Mama, you Win Immediately!

Other Rules / Explanations

If you ever get to gain Coins(s) and there are no Coins left, steal Coin(s) from the player with the most Coins. If you have the most Coins, do nothing.

If you ever get to gain food and there is no Food left, steal Food from the player with the most Food. If you have the most Food, do nothing. *See Pig for Exception

If you ever get to draw cards and there are none left, do nothing.
